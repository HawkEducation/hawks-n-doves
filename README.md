🦅 Bird Evolution Simulator
A web-based simulation of population dynamics using the Hawk-Dove game model, expanded with genetic traits and ecological niches.

🧬 The Species Hierarchy
Dove (0H): Pure pacifists. They share food but are easily bullied.

Toucan (1H): Mostly chill. 25% aggression, highly stable survivors.

Hummingbird (2H): The "Neutral" middle-ground.

Crow (3H): Calculated aggressors. 75% aggression, often "cooks" the competition.

Hawk (4H): Pure chaos. 100% aggression, prone to population crashes.

🍎 Ecological Mechanics
Beak Specialization: Birds evolve to eat Hard Nuts, Soft Berries, or become Finches (Generalists) that can eat both.

Habitat Selection: Specialized birds prefer their own tree types, creating "peaceful suburbs" vs. "war zones."

Mutation: A 10% chance for offspring to flip their behavior or beak type, ensuring the gene pool never truly stagnates.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Frontend: HTML5 / CSS3

Logic: Vanilla JavaScript

Charts: Chart.js
